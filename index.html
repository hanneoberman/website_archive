Hanne I. Oberman, MSc

Currently, I’m working as a lecturer and research associate at Utrecht University’s Methodology and Statistics department.

For 50% of my appointment I get to teach and supervise students, mostly in topics related to Applied Data Science. The other 50% of my appointment is research time. I’m involved in the Missing Data group led by Stef van Buuren and Gerko Vink, and I assist projects guided by Thomas Debray at the Julius Center for Health Sciences and Primary Care.
